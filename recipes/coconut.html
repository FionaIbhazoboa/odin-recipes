Coconut Rice

Description

What you are looking at is our famous Nigerian Jollof rice. Multiple countries have attempted to recreate it but none can compete with the legendary dish of my country.

Ingredients

  • Tomatoes
  • Onion
  • Scotch Pepper
  • Bell Pepper
  • Meat Stock
  • Tin Tomatoes
  • Vegetable Oil
  • Spices:
    • Curry
    • Thyme
    • Chilly Pepper
    • Black Pepper
    • Salt
    • Garlic
    • Maggi

Steps

  1. Blend Tomatoes, Scotch pepper, Bell Pepper and Onions together
  2. Put in pot and heat until dry
  3. In new pot, pour vegetable oil, dice onion to fry. Once oil is heated, put onion in pot
  4. As onion gets brown, add tin Tomatoes and stir.
  5. Add meat stock then stir.
  6. Add blended tomatoe sauce
  7. Stir then add spice to taste.
  8. Put on meduim heat for 15 mins
